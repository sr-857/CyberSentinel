- **Grid Overlay:** Subtle 1px cyan grid lines diverging from the center to convey network monitoring.
- **Signal Waves:** Faint pulse rings emanating from the CyberSentinel logo symbolising threat detection.

3. **Right:** Stacked cards illustrating key modules with icons:
